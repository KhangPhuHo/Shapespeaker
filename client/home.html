Transforming Early Math Education

ShapeSpeak combines tactile learning with RFID technology to create an inclusive, interactive math experience for children ages 2–4, including visually impaired learners.

Our Mission

To revolutionize early childhood education by creating interactive learning tools that make math concepts accessible, engaging, and fun for all children, regardless of ability.

Interactive Learning Features

ShapeSpeak brings math to life through multi-sensory experiences

Tactile Shape Recognition

Each geometric piece has a unique RFID tag that identifies its shape when placed on the base, helping children learn through touch and sound.

Creative Assembly

Children combine shapes to create complete objects, triggering educational stories about simple arithmetic and geometric properties.

Inclusive Design

Special textures and audio feedback make ShapeSpeak equally effective for visually impaired children, promoting inclusive education.

Companion App

Our upcoming app will expand learning opportunities with educational games and interactive content for children and parents.

Cognitive Development

Enhances logical thinking, shape recognition, and problem-solving skills through playful, hands-on activities.

Vibrant Colors

Bright, attractive colors stimulate visual learning and make the experience more engaging for young children.

Innovative RFID Technology

ShapeSpeak leverages cutting-edge RFID (Radio–Frequency Identification) technology to create a seamless interactive learning experience.

  • Instant Feedback: Each shape piece contains an RFID tag that communicates with the base station
  • Secure Connection: Magnets ensure proper alignment while preventing accidental swallowing
  • Expandable System: New shape sets can be added without hardware changes
