AI Camera Recognition Technology

ShapeSpeak uses an overhead camera and AI image recognition to help children identify and combine shapes through visual cues and responsive feedback.

  • When a shape is placed on the base, the camera captures and sends the image to an AI system to identify it and play its corresponding sound.
  • Children can creatively assemble shapes into full objects. The AI recognizes these creations and plays encouraging sounds and educational stories.
  • The camera setup is optimized at 24cm height above the base, ensuring accurate scanning from top-down perspective.
  • Special textured pieces and voice feedback make this experience inclusive for visually impaired children.
